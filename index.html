FreeWire Heart Monitor

JCCS & SR Innovations are proud to offer the FreeWire Heart Monitor, a revolutionary wireless continuous ECG monitoring device with patient care and comfort at the forefront of the design.

JCCS & SR Innovations provide high quality, easy to use holter monitoring devices to medical centre, hospitals and specialized cardiac facilities. Using the latest advancements in technology to achieve the most accurate measurements of cardiac activity.

The key features include:

  • Three sizes to cater for all ages - small child, child and adult
  • Three settings for one, two or three channel recordings
  • Time settings ranging from 24 to 72 hours of monitoring

Product Overview

The FreeWire Heart Monitor is a medical tool used to monitor electrical and muscular activity of the heart. It is a wireless holter monitor that provides a continuous one, two or three channel Electrocardiography (ECG)

The FreeWire Heart Monitor has a lightweight slim line design that adheres to the skin on the chest. It is waterproof, enabling everyday activities such as showering, exercising and even swimming for up to one hour

With a recording capacity of up to 72 hours, it provides reliable and accurate measurements through the use of the latest advancements in technolog

The FreeWire Heart Monitor is easy to use and monitoring will begin as soon as contact is made with the skin

  • Adjust the time and channel settings
  • Peel off the backing
  • Apply to the patients skin

The one, two or three channel design has been proven to provide comparable results with the standard 12 lead ECG. Better quality data has been produced as large amounts of external noise have been cancelled out by removing the wires. Resulting in a reduced amount of data lost due to artefact from the wired device.
