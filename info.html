Product Details

The Free Wire Heart monitor recorder has revolutionalized the world of electrocardiograph monitoring by focusing on maximizing the patient’s convenience and comfort as a tool towards attaining better compliance. The snap, peel, stick on and go recorder has a patented rhythm detection electrode which maximizes the ease of application and allows it to remain fully functional even during hot weather where perspiration is a concern in regards to electrode application, making it advantageous over its competitors. This saves a lot of service time unlike most other wired devices making it more user friendly compared to traditional recorders making it more preferable in the market.

Being the only body wearable Holter recorder, the device has created a competitive edge in the cardiac monitoring market. Most devices have the capacity to monitor electrocardiographs for twelve to twenty hours. However, Free Wire Heart monitoring pushes this to as long as two weeks adding to the advantages a cardiologist will reap by advising their patient on using this recorder for prolonged reliable monitoring.

The device is water resistant a feature that lacks in most conventional recorders. This allows the patients to shower or even swim enjoying their normal life without the restrictions that accompany most other recorders. In addition to this, the device uses a wireless communication platform and is aesthetically pleasant for patients who wish to continue with their activities of daily living such as office work compared to many other recorders that necessitate wiring along the patient’s body.

The Free Wire Heart monitors by JCSS&SR comes in varying sizes allowing electrocardiograph monitoring among patients in a diverse range of ages from neonates, pediatric to the adult population. It is the only body worn device that records up to 3 channels of ECG using the JCSS&SR panted electrode. In addition, the device incorporates a revamped software management system that enables it to interface with most analysis software. This means that organizations do not have to replace their analysis systems if they are compatible with the recorders. With these numerous benefits unmatched in the market, it is obvious that this is the future of continuous ECG holter monitor recording. We submit that cardiologists globally will soon be advising their patients on the need to switch to this outstanding brand.

Target Market

JCCS & SR Innovations provide high quality, easy to use holter monitoring devices to medical centre, hospitals and specialized cardiac facilities. Using the latest advancements in technology to achieve the most accurate measurements of cardiac activity.

The main component of the FreeWire Heart Monitor is to help people that are in need of vital medical attention. This monitor as proposed by our company is an innovative and new approach to the already congested medical sales field that we believe will be a cut above the rest and will change the way the heart and surrounding areas can be read and recorded.

There are many fields that we believe this device will be an asset for and include but are not limited to:

  • Doctors
  • Cardiologists
  • Nurses
  • Physiotherapists
  • MET (Medical Emergency Team) Members
  • Ambulance staff
  • Police
  • Heart Trouble Related Patients (All Ages)

All these titles would be suitable to carry or use the FreeWire Heart monitor as they all attend situations where an ECG or heart/muscle function may need to be recorded or read in an attempt to either keep a patient safe, calm and/or in a healthy HR (Heart Rate) zone. These devices are beneficial to any patron that they are carried by. Our Audience doesn’t stop at just professionals, It is also aimed at any person/s that have consistent heart troubles but do not have time to always see a doctor or go to a hospital. The FreeWire Heart monitor allows the user to non invasively read their own ECG and monitor their vitals on the go, it provides practicality to an audience who often struggle with having the confidence to be active, this device allows them to be active whilst keeping an eye on their health.
